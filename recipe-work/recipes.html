Chicken Alfreddo

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 1 Cup of Heavy Cream
  • 1/2 Cup of Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic, Minced
  • 2 Tablespoons of Butter
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste
  • Fresh Parsley for Garnish

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked and golden brown. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer over medium heat. Let it cook for 2-3 minutes until it starts to thicken.
  5. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and continue to cook until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  6. Return the cooked chicken breasts to the skillet, coating them with the Alfredo sauce. Let it simmer for another 2 minutes to heat through.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot over pasta or rice.
